IoT Air Quality Monitor

Live CO₂/PM2.5/TVOC streaming over SSE with alert thresholds. Ingest via HTTP or MQTT; simulate readings instantly.

Open Demo

  1. Problem

    Indoor air quality is invisible; without real-time insights, CO₂ and PM2.5 rise unnoticed, impacting focus and health.

  2. Approach

    Simple sensor ingest over HTTP/MQTT feeds an Express backend with SSE; a clean UI charts CO₂/PM2.5/TVOC with alert thresholds.

  3. Impact

    Faster ventilation decisions, clearer trends, and configurable alerts reduce exposure and improve comfort and productivity.
